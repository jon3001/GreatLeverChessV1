Find Us

Venue

The Ukrainian Club, 99 Castle Street, Bolton, BL2 1JP. Phone: 01204 526038.

Directions

From Manchester and the South

Follow directions to Bolton via M60, M62 and M61. Take the right hand lanes on M61, signposted A666 Bolton. Continue forward onto St Peter's Way - A666 and follow for about 2.5 - 3 miles. Exit left at junction signposted "Bolton South and Bury", but remain in outside lane to the traffic signals at top and ready to turn right onto the A579 Bradford Street (signposted Bury A58). Continue through another set of lights immediately ahead (which return to the A666) and turn right at the next set of lights (approx.200m) onto Castle Street (B6209).

The Ukrainian Club is approx. 75m on the left. There is a car park and on-street parking is available

From the North

Follow the A666 south of Blackburn, through Darwen, and toward the centre of Bolton (approx. 11 miles). On passing the large B&Q store (on your left) adopt the central lane (of three) to go straight ahead at the lights. After approx. 500 metres a slip road joins the A666 and you should adopt the left lane in order to take the slip road (signposted Bury), turning left, at the top, onto the A579 Bradford Street and turn right at the next set of lights (approx. 200m) on to Castle Street (B6209).

The Ukrainian Club is approx. 75m on the left. There is a car park and on-street parking is also available.

For your return journey: After turning left onto Bradford Street, as there is no right turn directly back onto the A666, continue along the A579 for approx. 200m, and turn right just before the lights, whence you will turn right again (at the junction facing the "Shell garage") bringing you back down the opposite one-way road, to turn left onto the A666 thence adopting the central lane toward Blackburn.

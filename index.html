Welcome

Tweets by @greatleverchess

Welcome to the Great Lever Chess Club website. Our club meets regularly near the centre of Bolton (Lancashire/Greater Manchester) for social and competive chess. We welcome players of all abilities. Individual members also compete on the weekend congress scene and in county events.

We have a private room available for match play. For club nights and analysis we tend to relax in the bar/lounge area.

This season we are competing in the Manchester League (A Division), the Bolton League (Division 1) and also the East Lancashire League (Division 3).

History

Established over 30 years ago originally in the Great Lever area of Bolton, the club has enjoyed success both in local leagues and national competitions.

Club Nights

We meet fairly regularly for social evenings and a bit of fun chess. These can be a bit impromptu but where possible we'll publish dates here in advance. Tuesday is our usual club night. Thursday is our match night for home fixtures in the Manchester league.

If you're new to the club and want to visit us we recommend that you contact us first so that we can ensure that someone will be available to meet you.

Failing that - check the fixtures and pop in during a home match night to chat to someone. Before the matches get underway at 7.30 pm is best.
